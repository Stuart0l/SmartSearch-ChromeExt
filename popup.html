How it works

This extension automatically detects if Google is accessible and routes your searches accordingly:

  • Google accessible: Searches go directly to Google
  • Google blocked: Searches are redirected to your configured fallback engine

The extension checks Google accessibility at the configured interval and caches the result for fast performance.
